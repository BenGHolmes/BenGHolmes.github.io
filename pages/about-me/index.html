About Me

Hi, I'm Ben. I'm a third year student at the University of British Columbia, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Engineering Physics, a highly competitive program that focuses on developing a foundation of high level math and physics knowledge, while also allowing me to customize my degree with a wide range of technical electives.

Recently, I have become fascinated by the field of data science - specifically it's applications to the world of business and finance. I think that attempting to model and predict such a complicated system would be an exciting challenge, and would fit well with the type of problem solving that I enjoy and excel at. I've taken my first steps towards a career in this area by taking online courses in statistics, data analysis, and financial modeling. I've applied the skills that I've learned to several projects on Kaggle, and to a data science competition hosted by UBC's Sauder School of Business. You can see more details about these on my projects page.

I'm still exploring my options in these fields, so if you're interested in collaborating on a project or sharing your experience in data science, finance, or management consulting, I'd love to connect. Please reach out to me on LinkedIn! A link to my page is in the site menu.
