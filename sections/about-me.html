About Me

I have a passion for software development, circuits, physics, and math. Given my endless curiosity and love for problem solving, I hope to build a career in either research and academia or entrepreneurship. I have taken my first step in this direction by pursuing a degree in Engineering Physics, an extremely competitive program that focuses on developing a foundation of high level math and physics knowledge, while also allowing me to customize my degree with a wide range of technical electives.

In addition to my coursework, I am constantly coming up with ideas for new projects to work on. Right now I'm working on a self driving RC car that uses a Raspberry Pi, a camera, and machine learning to navigate a taped course, and an Arduino-based, hardware password manager that securely stores login information offline and automatically enters it when connected to a computer.

In what little free time remains, I'm an avid climber, skier, and backpacker, and I dabble in biking, running and photography.
